Motivation

Why Time Series?
Why Africa?

Africa carries a disproportionate disease burden yet generates the least health AI research. Most health signals are not static snapshots — they are sequences. Ignoring time throws away the most important information a clinician has.

🦟

Malaria Outbreak Cycles

Seasonal rainfall patterns create predictable — and preventable — epidemic windows. Time series models can predict surges 4–6 weeks ahead.

🏥

ICU Deterioration

Vital signs sampled every hour contain rich temporal patterns. Models reading 72h of vitals outperform single-snapshot risk scores.

💊

Treatment Adherence

HIV/TB outcomes are driven by adherence trajectories over months — a time series problem, not a single measurement.

📡

Remote Monitoring

Wearable and mobile health signals from rural areas generate continuous streams that need intelligent, low-bandwidth analysis.

# ── Task 2: Sliding Window Baseline ──────────────────────────────
import numpy as np
import pandas as pd
from xgboost import XGBClassifier
from sklearn.metrics import roc_auc_score, average_precision_score

def sliding_window_features(ts: np.ndarray, window: int = 12) -> np.ndarray:
    """Extract statistical features over a rolling window."""
    features = []
    for i in range(window, len(ts)):
        w = ts[i - window : i]
        features.append([
            np.mean(w, axis=0),   # mean per channel
            np.std(w, axis=0),    # variability
            np.min(w, axis=0),    # trough
            np.max(w, axis=0),    # peak
            w[-1] - w[0],        # trend slope (crude)
        ])
    return np.array([np.concatenate(f) for f in features])

# Train and evaluate
X_train = sliding_window_features(train_vitals)
model = XGBClassifier(n_estimators=200, max_depth=6, use_label_encoder=False)
model.fit(X_train, y_train)

print(f"AUPRC: {average_precision_score(y_test, preds):.3f}")
Concept Map

What Participants
Will Learn

A progressive ladder from classical methods to frontier research.

📈

ARIMA & Classical Methods

Disease incidence forecasting. Understanding stationarity, differencing, autocorrelation functions.

🔄

LSTM / GRU Networks

Sequential modeling of patient vitals. Vanishing gradients, gating mechanisms, when depth helps.

🌊

Temporal Conv Networks

Dilated causal convolutions for long-range dependencies. Faster training than RNNs on clinical data.

⚡

Temporal Fusion Transformer

Multi-horizon forecasting with interpretable attention. State-of-the-art on multivariate health time series.

🎲

Conformal Prediction

Distribution-free uncertainty sets. Clinically meaningful coverage guarantees without Bayesian assumptions.

🔒

Federated Learning

Training across siloed hospital systems without moving patient data. Directly applicable to African health networks.
