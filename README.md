# 🧠 Temporal Health Intelligence

> **Using AI & Time Series Analysis to Predict and Prevent Disease**
> Deep Learning Indaba Workshop · 2 Hours · Hands-On

---

## Welcome

Africa carries a disproportionate disease burden yet generates the least health AI research. This workshop teaches you to use time series AI methods on real-world health signals — from wearables to epidemiological data — to build systems that actually matter on the continent.

**The core insight:** Most health signals are not static snapshots. They are *sequences*. Ignoring time throws away the most important information a clinician has.

---

## What You Will Build

By the end of this 2-hour workshop, you will have:

- Explored and visualized real ICU patient vital sign data as time series
- Built a **sliding-window XGBoost baseline** for patient deterioration prediction
- Upgraded to an **LSTM model** and understood when sequential models help (and when they don't)
- Applied **conformal prediction** for clinically meaningful uncertainty quantification
- Understood **open research problems** you can take home and publish on
