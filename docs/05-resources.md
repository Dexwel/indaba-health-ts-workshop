## Datasets

| Dataset | Description | Access |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| [PhysioNet Sepsis Challenge 2019](https://physionet.org/content/challenge-2019/1.0.0/) | 40,000+ ICU records, 40 features, hourly | Free (registration) |
| [MIMIC-III](https://physionet.org/content/mimiciii/1.4/) | Full ICU EHR, 60,000+ patients | Free (CITI training) |
| [WHO Disease Surveillance](https://www.who.int/data/gho) | Global epidemiological time series | Public |
| [Africa CDC Data](https://africacdc.org/resources/) | African-specific outbreak data | Public |

## Papers to Read

- **[Temporal Fusion Transformer (Lim et al., 2021)](https://arxiv.org/abs/1912.09363)** — Interpretable multi-horizon forecasting
- **[Sepsis Prediction from Clinical Notes (Moor et al., 2021)](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41746-021-00461-0)** — Clinical time series + NLP fusion
- **[Conformal Risk Control (Angelopoulos et al., 2023)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2208.02814)** — Rigorous uncertainty for health AI
- **[Federated Learning in Healthcare (Rieke et al., 2020)](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41746-020-00323-1)** — Overview of FL for medical imaging and EHR
